Human sperm development dataset from the human protein atlas:

- Download and unzip the single cell dataset rna_single_cell_read_count.zip from https://www.proteinatlas.org/humanproteome/single+cell/single+cell+type/data#datasets
- Place the two testis datasets rna_single_cell_read_count/testis/cell_data.tsv & rna_single_cell_read_count/testis/read_count.tsv into the folder Data/Guo_et_al/OriginalData
- For cluster annotation, download and unzip the cluster dataset rna_single_cell_cluster.tsv.zip from https://www.proteinatlas.org/humanproteome/single+cell/single+cell+type/data#datasets, plse into the folder Data/Guo_et_al/OriginalData
